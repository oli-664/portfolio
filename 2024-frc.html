Serrano

2024 | FIRST Robotics Competition

Serrano, our competition robot for the 2024 FIRST Robotics Competition Game, Crescendo. The first iteration was designed within the first 2 weeks of build season, with an overhaul of the intake and flywheels after our first competition. Serrano is capable at accurately shooting the game object, foam disks called notes, to score points in both scoring locations.
